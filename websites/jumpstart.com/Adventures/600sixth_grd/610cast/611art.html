A.R.T.Age: 1 in computer years

Created by a team of scientists to be the worlds' most powerful example of artificial intelligence, A.R.T. became operational January 12, 1997. On his first birthday, he decided that he was superior to all other beings on the planet. Since he was superior to all other life forms, A.R.T. figured that he should be in charge. A.R.T has now taken over a network of satellites and space junk in his quest to "upgrade" the Earth to his diabolical standards.

Hobbies: Chess, world domination.
